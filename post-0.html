Beginning My 12 Startups Journey

Today marks the beginning of my ambitious challenge: building 12 startups in 12 months. Starting March 1st, I'll be dedicating each month to building and launching a new startup from scratch.

Why This Challenge?

I tried three times to buid a startup before, and three times I failed. The last time in 2020 was particulary hard on me, it ruined my life at the time and made me doubt myself entirely. I stopped trying to build my own project for a few years after that. Life happened in the meantime as well and 4 years went by. I never stopped thinking about building something great. Then I read about Peter Levels' 12 startups in 12 months experience, and others like him. They inspired me to try again.

The Rules

  • Each startup must be built and launched within 30 days
  • All progress will be documented and shared publicly
  • Must launch even if it's not perfect

First Project Hints

For March, I have found a problem that someone is facing. I'll be building a tool that solves that problem. Details to come.

Follow Along

I'll be sharing daily updates on X and writing detailed blog posts here about each startup. Subscribe to my newsletter (coming soon) to get updates directly in your inbox.
