Beginning My 12 Startups Journey

Today marks the beginning of my ambitious challenge: building 12 startups in 12 months. Starting March 1st, I'll be dedicating each month to building and launching a new startup from scratch. This journey is about learning, building in public, and pushing my limits as a maker.

Why This Challenge?

As a software engineer, I've spent years building things for others. Now, I want to challenge myself to build and launch my own products. The goal isn't just to build 12 startups - it's to learn about different markets, experiment with various technologies, and understand what it takes to launch successful products.

The Rules

  • Each startup must be built and launched within 30 days
  • All progress will be documented and shared publicly
  • Each project must have a clear revenue model
  • Must launch even if it's not perfect

What to Expect

Throughout this journey, I'll be documenting everything - from ideation to launch, including:

  • Daily progress updates
  • Technical decisions and challenges
  • Marketing strategies and results
  • Revenue and metrics
  • Lessons learned

First Project Hints

For March, I already have a clear idea of what I'll be building. It's a tool that solves a problem I've personally experienced as a developer. More details coming soon!
